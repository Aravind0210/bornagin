Our industries

With more than 80 years of experience in manufacturing industrial rubber and a stable clientele from dozens of different industries, we developed technological solutions to meet industry standards and unique, specific requirements. Always. Perfectly.

Machining

Key for vibration control, shock absorption and integral parts in conveyor systems.

Agriculture

Ensuring durability, flexibility and essential components for agricultural machinery.

Marine industry

Electronics

Insulation, absorption and sealing in electronic devices and equipment.

Transportation
