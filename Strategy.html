Industry Insights

Understanding the challenges industries especially rubber units face in today’s economy.

What is Industrial Sickness?

A state where businesses face operational or financial breakdowns due to:

  • • Mounting debts
  • • Obsolete technologies
  • • Declining market demand
  • • Regulatory & environmental pressures

Specific to Rubber Industry

  • • Health hazards → absenteeism → production loss
  • • High raw material prices
  • • Import/export bottlenecks
  • • Environmental non-compliance
  • • Inability to scale or diversify
